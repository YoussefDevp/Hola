Frequently Asked Questions

What is HALO?

HALO is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more on thousands of internet-connected devices.

You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want without a single commercial – all for one low monthly price. There's always something new to discover and new TV shows and movies are added every week!

How much does HALO cost?

Watch HALO on your smartphone, tablet, Smart TV, laptop, or streaming device, all for one fixed monthly fee. Plans range from EUR7.99 to EUR15.99 a month. No extra costs, no contracts.

Where can I watch?

Watch anywhere, anytime, on an unlimited number of devices. Sign in with your HALO account to watch instantly on the web at HALO.com from your personal computer or on any internet-connected device that offers the HALO app, including smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, streaming media players and game consoles.

You can also download your favorite shows with the iOS, Android, or Windows 10 app. Use downloads to watch while you're on the go and without an internet connection. Take HALO with you anywhere.

How do I cancel?

HALO is flexible. There are no pesky contracts and no commitments. You can easily cancel your account online in two clicks. There are no cancellation fees – start or stop your account anytime.

HALO has an extensive library of feature films, documentaries, TV shows, anime, award-winning HALO originals, and more. Watch as much as you want, anytime you want.
